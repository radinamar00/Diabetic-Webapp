Tomato Soup

Information

Type: Soup, Hot dishes
Nutritions: Energy 1450 kJ, Carbohydrate 25 g, Fat 14 g
Nr. of persons: 1 person

Ingridients

½ leeks (about 60 g)
1 small carrot (about 50 g)
1 slice of cabbage (about 200 g)
75 g minced beef with a maximum of 10% fat
1 tsp. liquid fat
2½ dl vegetable broth
½ ds. peeled tomatoes (200 g)
½ tbsp. tomato paste, preferably concentrate
½ tsp. mild pepper
salt, pepper

Preparation

Cut the leek into thin rings. Cut the carrot into small cubes. Cut the cabbage finely.
Brown the meat in the fat. Add the vegetables and sweat them. Add the remaining ingredients and cook the soup for 10-15 minutes. Taste the soup for.
Serving
Serve tomato soup with whole grain bread.
Alternative
You can replace the carrot or some of the cabbage with celery.
